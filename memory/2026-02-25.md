### 💡 Lessons Learned

1. **3CX SBC acts as bridge** between local devices and 3CX Cloud
2. **SBC doesn't automatically route locally** - it forwards everything to Cloud
3. **FreeSWITCH is the actual SIP engine** - voice-app just controls it
4. **drachtio is the control channel** - must authenticate with correct secret
5. **env_file overrides environment variables** in docker-compose
6. **DEBUG logging essential** for troubleshooting SIP issues
